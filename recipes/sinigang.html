Quick Sinigang na Baboy (Tamarind Soup)

Serves 2–3

Ingredients

  • 300 g pork ribs or shoulder, small pieces
  • 1 small onion, quartered
  • 1 tomato, quartered
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste or sinigang sachet
  • 1 small radish, sliced
  • A handful of kangkong or bok choy
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • Fish sauce, to taste

Steps

  1. Boil pork with onion & tomato, skim foam; simmer 20 min.
  2. Add radish & tamarind, simmer 5 min.
  3. Season with fish sauce; adjust tanginess.
  4. Add greens & chili, cook 1–2 min until wilted.
  5. Ladle into bowls & enjoy.
