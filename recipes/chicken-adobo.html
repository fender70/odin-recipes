Mini Chicken Adobo

Serves 2–3

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken thigh pieces (skin‑on)
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup white vinegar
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce, garlic, bay leaf, peppercorns, and sugar for 15 min.
  2. Sear chicken in a hot pan until lightly browned, about 3 min per side.
  3. Pour in the marinade and vinegar; let sizzle 1 min before stirring.
  4. Add water, bring to a simmer, cover, and cook 20 min on low.
  5. Uncover and reduce sauce to a glaze, 5–8 min.
  6. Serve over hot rice.
