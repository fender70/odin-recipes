Petite Chicken Tinola

Serves 2–3

Ingredients

  • 300 g chicken breast or thighs, bite‑sized
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, julienned
  • 2 cups water or chicken stock
  • 1 small green papaya or chayote, sliced
  • A handful of chili leaves or spinach
  • Fish sauce, to taste (start with 1 tsp)

Steps

  1. Sauté onion, garlic & ginger in oil until fragrant (~2 min).
  2. Add chicken; cook until opaque (~3 min).
  3. Pour in water/stock, bring to boil, skim foam.
  4. Lower heat, add papaya/chayote, simmer 10 min.
  5. Season with fish sauce.
  6. Stir in greens until wilted; serve hot.
