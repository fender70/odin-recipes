Tiny Dinuguan (Pork Blood Stew)

Serves 2–3

Ingredients

  • 300 g pork belly or shoulder, cubed
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup pork blood, mixed with ½ cup water
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1–2 bird’s eye chilies (optional)
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Brown pork in dry pan until fat renders (~5 min). Drain excess fat.
  2. Sauté onion & garlic in pork fat until soft.
  3. Return pork, add 1 cup water, simmer 15 min until tender.
  4. Stir in blood mixture continuously to avoid clots.
  5. Add vinegar & chilies, simmer 5 min.
  6. Season with salt & pepper; serve with rice or puto.
