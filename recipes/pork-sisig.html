Small‑Batch Pork Sisig

Serves 2–3

Ingredients

  • 250 g pork belly, boiled & chopped
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1–2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp calamansi juice (or lemon)
  • 1 bird’s eye chili, minced (optional)
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Pan‑fry pork until crisp; drain excess fat.
  2. Lower heat, stir in onion until translucent.
  3. Push to one side; add mayo & calamansi, scramble briefly.
  4. Mix everything, season with salt, pepper & chili.
  5. Serve sizzling or straight off the pan.
